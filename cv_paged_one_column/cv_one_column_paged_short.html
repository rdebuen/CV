Personal Summary

I am a graduate student in Public Policy and Governance. My interests lie in the application of qualitative and quantitative methods to questions at the intersection of environmental and urban mobility policy. My current research broadly examines (1) how different types of shocks to the transportation system impact cycling, transit and bikeshare use and 2) the emergence of governance regimes for promoting cycling in Mexican cities.
